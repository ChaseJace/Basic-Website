About us!

Founded on 2012, DenDen Laundry Shoppe is a full-service laundry
located at Dona Seguna Building. Ponciano. Wherein the establishment
washes, dries, and folds the clothes dropped off by the customers.
The laundry shoppe normally caters to students and employees living near schools and offices.
